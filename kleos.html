Kleos System

MARK's advanced reasoning engine that transforms your prompts into optimized queries for superior AI responses.

What is Kleos?

Kleos is MARK's advanced capabilities engine. It transforms MARK from a simple chatbot into a reasoning agent by forcing a "Think before you speak" workflow.

Why use Kleos?

Standard LLMs often rush to answer, missing context or nuance. Kleos addresses this by analyzing your request, refining it, and then executing it with optimized precision.

Deeper Analysis

Examines your request for missing context or hidden requirements

Prompt Optimization

Acts as an expert "Prompt Engineer" to refine your query

Superior Results

Delivers drastically higher quality answers and deeper insights

How Kleos Works

1

Analysis Phase

Kleos analyzes your prompt for ambiguity, missing context, or hidden requirements. It identifies what information is needed to provide the best possible answer.

Example:

If you ask "Explain neural networks," Kleos might ask clarifying questions like:

  • Are you looking for a beginner-friendly explanation or a technical deep dive?
  • Do you want to focus on the mathematical aspects or practical implementation?
  • Is this for a specific domain like computer vision or NLP?
2

User Clarification

You respond to Kleos' clarifying questions, providing the additional context needed for a tailored response.

Interaction:

Kleos presents its questions, and you provide answers. This creates a collaborative process to refine your request.

3

Prompt Generation

Based on your inputs, Kleos generates an optimized "Master Prompt" that captures the essence of what you're looking for.

Result:

You can review and confirm the generated prompt, or request modifications before proceeding.

4

Deep Reasoning

Kleos uses the optimized prompt with the AI model, applying deep reasoning techniques to generate a comprehensive, high-quality response.

Outcome:

You receive a detailed, well-structured response that addresses your specific needs with appropriate depth and context.

Usage Examples

Technical Explanation

/kleos Explain how to build a neural network from scratch

MARK will pause, analyze your request, ask clarifying questions about your technical level and specific interests, generate an optimized prompt, and then deliver a superior tutorial tailored to your needs.

Code Generation

/kleos Write a Python script to analyze sales data with specific requirements

Kleos will ask about your data format, required outputs, and specific algorithms you want to use before generating optimized code.

Research Assistance

/kleos Compare different approaches to solving the traveling salesman problem

Kleos will clarify whether you need a theoretical comparison, practical implementation advice, or both, then provide a comprehensive analysis.

Tips for Effective Kleos Usage

Be Specific Initially

While Kleos will ask clarifying questions, starting with more detail helps generate better questions.

Engage with Questions

Provide thoughtful answers to Kleos' questions to get the most tailored response.

Review Generated Prompts

Take a moment to review the Master Prompt before execution to ensure it captures your intent.

Use for Complex Queries

Kleos is most beneficial for complex, multi-faceted questions where context matters.
